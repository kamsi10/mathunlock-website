Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 2025

1. Introduction

MathUnlock ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard information when you use our mobile application.

2. Information We Collect

MathUnlock is designed with privacy in mind. We collect minimal information:

  • App Usage Data: Statistics about your math challenges (problems solved, accuracy, etc.) are stored locally on your device.
  • Subscription Information: Subscription status is managed through Apple's App Store and RevenueCat. We do not store payment information.
  • Device Information: Basic device information may be collected by Apple and RevenueCat for subscription management.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide and improve our app's functionality
  • Manage your subscription through Apple's App Store
  • Display statistics and progress within the app

4. Data Storage

All app data, including statistics and settings, is stored locally on your device. We do not transmit or store your personal data on our servers. Subscription information is handled by Apple and RevenueCat in accordance with their privacy policies.

5. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Apple App Store: For subscription management and app distribution
  • RevenueCat: For subscription entitlement management

These services have their own privacy policies governing data collection and use.

6. Children's Privacy

MathUnlock is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your data (stored locally on your device)
  • Delete your data by uninstalling the app
  • Manage your subscription through Apple's App Store settings

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this page.
